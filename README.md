# e-commerce_sales_data_analysis

## Project Type
Exploratory Data Analysis

## Contribution
Individual

## Project Introduction
This project focuses on analyzing an e-commerce dataset to derive valuable insights into customer behavior, sales trends, and seller performance. The dataset, a comprehensive collection of customer, order, product, payment, and seller information, provides a rich ground for exploratory data analysis and understanding various facets of the online retail ecosystem.

## Objectives
*   **Understand Customer Demographics and Spending Patterns**: Analyze customer distribution across cities and states, and evaluate their purchasing habits over time.
*   **Identify Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)**: Explore metrics such as total sales, monthly sales trends, order status distribution, and delivery times.
*   **Assess Product and Seller Performance**: Determine top-selling product categories, analyze revenue generated by individual sellers, and investigate correlations between product attributes and purchase frequency.
*   **Optimize Business Strategies**: Provide data-driven recommendations for targeted marketing, inventory management, and customer retention based on the insights gained.

Through various data manipulation techniques, statistical analysis, and data visualization, this project aims to uncover patterns and relationships that can inform strategic business decisions for an e-commerce platform.

## Dataset Description
This project utilizes a comprehensive e-commerce dataset composed of several interconnected tables. Key tables and attributes include:
*   **Customers**: `customer_id`, `customer_unique_id`, `customer_zip_code_prefix`, `customer_city`, `customer_state`
*   **Orders**: `order_id`, `customer_id`, `order_status`, `order_purchase_timestamp`, `order_approved_at`, `order_delivered_carrier_date`, `order_delivered_customer_date`, `order_estimated_delivery_date`
*   **Order Items**: `order_id`, `order_item_id`, `product_id`, `seller_id`, `shipping_limit_date`, `price`, `freight_value`
*   **Payments**: `order_id`, `payment_sequential`, `payment_type`, `payment_installments`, `payment_value`
*   **Products**: `product_id`, `product_category`, `product_name_length`, `product_description_length`, `product_photos_qty`, `product_weight_g`, `product_length_cm`, `product_height_cm`, `product_width_cm`
*   **Sellers**: `seller_id`, `seller_zip_code_prefix`, `seller_city`, `seller_state`

## Data Cleaning and Preprocessing
1.  **Identify Missing Values**: Initial assessment of null values in the dataset.
2.  **Handle Critical Missing Values**: Removed rows with missing values in `order_item_id`, `product_id`, `seller_id`, `price`, and `freight_value` to ensure data integrity for transactional analysis.
3.  **Remove Duplicate Rows**: Ensured uniqueness of observations by removing exact duplicate rows.
4.  **Convert Data Types**: Converted all relevant timestamp columns (`order_purchase_timestamp`, `order_approved_at`, `order_delivered_carrier_date`, `order_delivered_customer_date`, `order_estimated_delivery_date`, `shipping_limit_date`) to datetime objects for accurate time-series analysis.
5.  **Feature Engineering**: Created new features:
    *   `sales`: Calculated as `price + freight_value`.
    *   `Year` and `Month`: Extracted from `order_purchase_timestamp`.
    *   `delivery_days`: Calculated as the difference between `order_delivered_customer_date` and `order_purchase_timestamp`.

## Key Findings and Insights

### Customer Demographics and Spending Patterns
*   **Geographical Concentration**: São Paulo (SP) is the dominant customer hub, leading in unique customers across cities and states. 'sao paulo' and 'rio de janeiro' were the leading cities by customer count, and 'SP' had the overwhelming majority of unique customers among states.
*   **Purchasing Behavior**: Most orders consist of a single item, indicating a preference for individual product purchases. Some smaller cities showed a higher average number of items per order, suggesting localized purchasing habits.
*   **Payment Preferences**: 'Credit Card' was overwhelmingly the most used payment method, contributing the highest amount of revenue. Approximately 51.5% of orders utilized installment payments, highlighting the popularity of flexible payment options.

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
*   **Sales Trends**: Monthly sales showed an upward trend from late 2016 to mid-2018, with noticeable seasonal peaks, particularly towards the end of the year. 2018 recorded the highest order volume.
*   **Order Status**: The vast majority of orders were successfully 'delivered', indicating efficient fulfillment, with a minimal number of 'canceled' orders.
*   **Delivery Times**: The average delivery time was around 12 days, with most deliveries occurring within a reasonable timeframe, though some outliers took longer.
*   **Order Price Distribution**: Most orders fall into lower price ranges, suggesting a high volume of lower-cost goods or a customer base that is more price-sensitive.

### Product and Seller Performance
*   **Top Product Categories**: 'HEALTH BEAUTY', 'Watches present', and 'bed table bath' consistently ranked as the top categories by both total sales and revenue percentage, driving the most revenue for the platform.
*   **Product Price vs. Purchases**: A very weak, almost negligible negative correlation (-0.03) was observed between product price and the number of purchases. This implies that product price alone doesn't strongly dictate purchase frequency, suggesting other factors (e.g., product utility, marketing, brand) are more influential.
*   **Top Sellers**: Specific seller IDs were identified as leading revenue generators, indicating high-performing partners who contribute significantly to the platform's sales.

## Business Strategies & Recommendations
*   **Targeted Marketing**: Focus campaigns on high-concentration areas like São Paulo, and tailor product promotions based on top-selling categories.
*   **Payment Optimization**: Emphasize and optimize installment payment options to further boost sales and customer satisfaction.
*   **Logistics & Customer Satisfaction**: Investigate causes of longer delivery outliers to enhance customer experience. Monitor order cancellation reasons to improve operational processes.
*   **Product Portfolio**: Consider strategies for either increasing the volume of lower-priced items or strategically introducing higher-priced products with value-added services.
*   **Seller Engagement**: Support and recognize top-performing sellers to foster loyalty, and mentor underperforming sellers to improve overall platform revenue.

This analysis provides a solid foundation for data-driven decision-making, enabling the e-commerce platform to understand its market, optimize operations, and enhance customer satisfaction.
